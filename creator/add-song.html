3 · Review, correct, and translate each line

Words were split and tagged automatically from the bundled CC-CEDICT + HSK 3.0 data. Merge joins a word with the one after it if the split was wrong. Split breaks a word into its first character and the rest. 3.5 · Constructions (optional)

For grammar patterns split across non-adjacent words (like 在...之前), click Link words, then click each word that's part of the pattern in the review area above. Save once you've picked them all. Linked words get an underline in the published reader, color-coded per pattern.

4 · Publish

Publish writes this song directly into the site's database — no files, no redeploying. If its status is set to Draft above, only your signed-in creator account can see it; switch to Published whenever you want it live for everyone. The backup JSON is just a local copy for your own records, not something you need to do anything with.
